How to Run it in Your Tesla

1 Download Pack

Generate and download the **Bundle ZIP** containing **`lightshow.fseq`** and **`lightshow.wav`** directly.

2 Copy to USB

Extract the files and copy them into a folder named **`LightShow`** at the root of a FAT32/exFAT USB flash drive.

3 Plug & Play

Plug the USB into your Tesla Toybox, select Light Show, step out, close the doors, and watch the show!
