How to Run Your Custom Light Show

1 Map & Convert

Drag & drop your Beat Saber map ZIP or folder. Choose a difficulty, preview the 3D show in real-time, and generate your Bundle ZIP.

2 Copy to USB

Extract your files (`lightshow.fseq` and `lightshow.wav`) and copy them into a folder exactly named LightShow at the root of a FAT32 or exFAT USB flash drive.

3 Play in Tesla

Plug the USB into your Tesla. Navigate to Toybox → Light Show. Select the USB custom show option, step out of the car, close the doors, and enjoy!
